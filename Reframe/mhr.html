MENTAL HEALTH
RESOURCES

AnxietyBC

AnxietyBC is an organization based out of British Columbia. Their mandate is to
help people with anxiety problems. Their website is excellent for finding more
information about anxiety and other methods for dealing with anxiety, outside
of what we covered.

Go to site

Anxiety Disorders Association of Canada

A national center focused on all anxiety disorders. This website offers more
information on the various anxiety disorders, and resources for coping with
anxiety. There is a student portal for youth which offers much more information
for youth facing anxiety.

Go to site

Canadian Mental Health Association

The CMHA is a national resource for all mental health. Going onto their website,
you can locate your local CMHA chapter and gain more information about the
programs and initiatives that they have.

Go to site

Greater Good

A resource for learning more about mindfulness and mindfulness practices.
Edited by the University of Berkeley in California, Greater Good offers a lot of
resources on mindfulness practices. If you would like to learn more about being
aware and keeping in the now, this is a great resource.

Go to site

IWK Mental Health

The IWK is a hospital located in Nova Scotia. Their Website offers many
resources about coping with mental health problems and about what
resources may be available. They are great for getting more information about
anxiety and other related mental health concerns.

Go to site

Kids Help Phone

1-800-668-6868
A national program aimed at helping youth in need. If there is a problem you
may be facing emotionally and need some immediate help to talk about it, this
is a service for you. Kids Help Phone offers phone counselling service for youth
and keeps your information secret. No one else will find out about your
conversation, you do not even have to tell them your name. They are there to
help you.

Go to site
